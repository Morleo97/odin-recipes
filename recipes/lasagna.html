Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Recipe

  • minced beef
  • onion
  • green or red pepper
  • mushrooms
  • chopped tomatoes
  • tomato sauce
  • garlic
  • cheese sauce
  • lasagne sheets
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
